Brown University Metropolis Theme

vskbellala/metropolis-brown

Brown University color scheme for Metropolis LaTeX beamer theme.

  • Colors extracted from Brown University Visual Identity

Usage

  1. Copy metropolisbrown.sty to your project.
  2. In your main.tex, set up Metropolis and load the package.
% Preamble
\documentclass[10pt]{beamer}
\usetheme[progressbar=frametitle]{metropolis}
\usepackage{appendixnumberbeamer}
\usepackage{metropolisbrown} % Don't forget to load this!
  1. Enjoy your presentation!
