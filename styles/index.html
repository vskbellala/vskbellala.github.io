About This Page

This page contains samples of each element that I have stylized and would put into my webpage. It serves as a personal reference.

Structure

This website is divided into 2 sections: "main" and "article." The main section is located at the top of the page, a dark banner spanning the entire viewport. The article section also spans the entire viewport and comes after the main section. It's width has also been modified to be only 50% of the viewport's width on non-mobile screens to improve readability. Each section has their own color scheme.
